About

What is it?

The Student Society for News Design is having a contest for all college design students to get recognition and awarded for the work they have done whether for a campus publication, projects done for a class, a job or an internship.

We believe that it is important to give a voice to the voiceless. Often times, there are a number of student designers who have demonstrated incredible talent in the classroom or at a part-time job. But, these students rarely have the opportunity to showcase this talent.

So, each year we come together to share their work and emphasize the importance of storytelling across all mediums. This contest is about the countless stories they have told, the dedication of deadlines and the importance of ethical journalism.

We want all students to enter the contest and share your voice and your work.



Who can enter?

Currently enrolled college students

*Submit work from a college class, a student publication, an internship or a job that fits any of our 16 categories.

Entries will be judged by a panel of design professionals.
